Currently, I’m teaching workshops, directing lab projects, and creating educational programming for the Ahmanson Lab, an interdisciplinary makerspace at USC that encourages students and professors to think critically about technology. We offer access to a 3D printer, an OpenBCI EEG machine, microcontroller kits, a drone, and VR dev resources. I help professors and students use these technologies to tackle interesting problems, such as Mars terraforming experiments or creating an EEG based brain computer interface.
